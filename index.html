Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expense Manager. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Expense Manager is designed with a "Local First" approach. All financial data, categories, and budgets you create within the app are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage.

  • No Personal Financial Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit your individual transactions or account balances to any external servers.
  • Local Storage: Your data remains under your full control on your device.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the application. To provide relevant ads and improve their services, Google may collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA for iOS or Advertising ID for Android).
  • Location information (if permissions are granted).
  • Interaction data with ads.

For more details on how Google handles your information, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Security

Since your data is stored locally, its security depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level encryption, passwords, or biometric authentication to protect your information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our official GitHub repository or support channels.
